Prachi Bisht

I am a Satellite Systems Engineer and Data Scientist at the U R Rao Satellite Centre, a unit of the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO). I work in power systems and my work focuses on developing physics-informed models and machine learning frameworks for space-based solar energy forecasting and on-orbit system diagnostics. I operate at the intersection of remote sensing, statistical modeling, and optimization, with a broader goal of advancing reliable, data-driven solutions for space and renewable energy applications. Lately, my interests have evolved toward ML-driven Earth Observation and Space Weather applications.
