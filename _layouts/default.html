Prachi Bisht

I am a Satellite Systems Engineer and Data Scientist at the U R Rao Satellite Centre, a unit of the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO). I work in power systems and my work focuses on developing physics-informed models and machine learning frameworks for space-based solar energy forecasting, space weather and on-orbit system diagnostics. Lately, my interests have evolved toward ML-driven Earth Observation and space-based edge inferencing.
